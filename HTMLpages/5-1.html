Works of Vincent Van Gogh

The Starry Night

Vicent van Gogh painted Starry Night in 1889 during his stay at the asylum of Saint-Paul-de-Mausole near Saint-Remy-de-Provence.Van Gogh lived well in the hospitals; he was allowed more freedoms than any of the other patients .if attended he could leave the hospitals grounds he was allowed to paint ,read ,and withdraw into his own room.
Van Gogh's paintings of sun flowers are among his most famous.He did them in Arles,in the south of france in 1888 and 1889.Vincent painted a total of five large canvases with sunflowers in a vase with three shades of yellow 'and nothing else'.In this way , he demonstrated that it was possible to create an image with numerous variations of a single color without any loss of eloquence
