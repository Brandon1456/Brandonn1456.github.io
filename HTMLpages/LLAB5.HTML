Almond Green Salad

Fresh and Crispy

Ingredients

1/2 pound bacon
2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
3 tabelspoons honey
1/2 teaspoons dry hot mustard
1/2 teaspoons celery salt
1/2 teaspoons ground paprika
1/4 cup olive oil
1 head red leaf lettuce torn into bite-size pieces
1 (15 ounce) can of mandarin oranges drained
1 bunch green onions diced
3/4 cup slivered almonds

Directions

1. in medium skillet over medium-heat cook bacon until evenly browned drained cool and crumble
2.to make the dressing thoroughly blend the vinegar, honey ,dry mustard,celery salt paprika and olive oil
3.place lettuce oranges, green onions,bacon,and almonds in serving bowl .Toss with dressing and serve
